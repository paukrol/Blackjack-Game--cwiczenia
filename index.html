BlackJack Dice Game Rules

  1. In each turn players roll two dice and add the numbers to their turn total. Players can roll as many times as they wish.
  2. Once players have scored 16 or more, they have the option to stop rolling or just roll one die one or more times.
  3. If their score goes over 21, then they bust and their turn score is zero.
  4. The aim is to score 21 or get closest to 21 without going over.
  5. Each player scoring 21 in the round gets 2 points.
  6. If no player scores 21 then the player closest to 21 gets 1 point. If more than one player have the same score, then no point is given to any player.
  7. The game is played for multiple rounds and the first player to reach 10 points wins the game.
